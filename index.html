About Me

My name is Giovanni Esposito. I’m a student at Fordham University where I study philosophy and stay heavily involved in theatre and performance. I’ve always been interested in the way ideas, stories, and people connect, and acting lets me bring those connections to life. This website is part of my HTML project, and I wanted it to reflect both my academic interests and my creative work.

I’ve performed in a variety of productions over the years — comedy, musicals, straight plays — and each role has taught me something different about timing, presence, and collaboration. When I’m not in rehearsal or class, I like exploring New York City, writing, and listening to old jazz singers.

My Main Interests

Some things I am currently focused on include:

  • Theatre — especially character roles and musical theatre
  • Philosophy, ethics, and understanding how people think
  • Acting training and improving my performance technique
  • Music, including jazz and classic vocal standards
  • Working with others in creative or community-based settings
